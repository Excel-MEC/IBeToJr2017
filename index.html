Event Details

Event Details

X

It is all pretty simple actually! All you need to do is:

  • Come up with an ingenious idea (which shouldn't be all that difficult being the little genius that you are!)
  • Fill in the details in the google form given in the official IBeTo website, ibeto.excelmec.org.
  • If your idea gets shortlisted, you get to work on your project and exhibit it at the national level techno-managerial symposium of Govt. Model Engineering College, Excel 2017, come this October!

What projects are we looking for?

Something a little more than the quintessential science project, something technically innovative and socially beneficial. Originality and creativity are what count! And yes, implementability too.

Incase you need it, we provide you with the technical help/advice and expertise to help you make your project idea a reality.

The Reward?

Chance to have your project exhibited at Excel 2017, which is to be held over 3 power packed days this October.

Oh! And not to forget the Rs. 25,000 the best project walks away with!
So, why wait? IBeTo-Junior knocks at your door!

Rules

Rules

X

General

  • The idea must be original, innovative, socially beneficial, and implementable as a project.

Eligibility & Team Specifications

  • Eligibility: School students (Grade 8 and upwards), not more than 19 years old.
  • The maximum number of members in a team is 5.
  • Cross-Institution teams are permitted.

Guidelines

  • The write-up about the idea should be sent in the specified format.
  • The Google form given in our website, ibeto.excelmec.org, should be filled out on or before July 10th, 2017.
  • The best teams will be selected, and these teams will have to implement their idea and develop their project, before Excel 2017.
  • The selected teams will be informed through telephone and e-mail. Team Excel 2017 will not be responsible for any case in which shortlisted teams are unavailable or unreachable for contact at any point of time after announcement of results.
  • In order to complete their projects, selected teams will be provided with technical help / guidance by Team Excel 2017, if they so wish.
  • Projects are to be completed by September 2017 and all completed products will be exhibited at Excel 2017.
  • All completed projects will be awarded a Certificate of Completion, authorized by supporting institutions, faculty and Team Excel 2017.
  • The best project will be awarded prize money worth INR 25,000.
  • Rules are liable to change, and teams will be informed of the same if any.
