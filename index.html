Innovations for a Better Tomorrow, The Junior edition

This is that platform that you’ve always wanted, to make your mark on society and do your bit to help the world.For age is but a number and an idea is ageless.

Rules

General

The idea must be original, innovative, socially beneficial, and implementable as a project.

Eligilibility and Team specification

Eligibility: School students (Grade 8 and upwards), not more than 19 years old. The maximum number of members in a team is 5. Cross-Institution teams are permitted.

Event Details

→ The Google form should be filled on or before June 30th, 2016

→ The best teams will be selected, and these teams will have to implement their idea and develop their project, before Excel 2016.

→ The selected teams will be informed through telephone and e-mail.

→ Team Excel 2016 will not be responsible for any case in which shortlisted teams are unavailable or unreachable for contact at any point of time after announcement of results.

→ In order to complete their projects, selected teams will be provided with technical help / guidance by Team Excel 2016, if they so wish.

→ Projects are to be completed by September 2016 and all completed products will be exhibited at Excel 2016.

→ All completed projects will be awarded a Certificate of completion, authorized by supporting institutions, faculty and Team Excel 2016.

→ The best projects will be awarded prize money of INR 25,000.

→ Rules are liable to change, and teams will be informed of the same if any.
